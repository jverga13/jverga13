# Hi, I'm John Patrick Verga 👋

**Computer Engineer | Robotics | Hardware-Software Integration**

I am a Computer Engineering honors student at the **University of Michigan** (B.S. '28) focused on bridging the gap between high-level autonomous decision-making and low-level hardware execution. 

Outside of class, I am the Research Lead for the Atombot Swarm Robotics & Collective Intelligence Lab (Z-Lab). In this role, I architect custom boards and firmware for our robots' compute stage and build LiDAR-camera SLAM systems for swarm robotics.
